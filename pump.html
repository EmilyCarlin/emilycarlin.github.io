PumpSense

Predictive Sump Pump Tracking

"PumpSense" is a tool made by a Boulder, Colorado based startup called Boulder Automation. If you are a homeowner with a basement, you probably know that your sump pump is what stands between your basement and flooding. The PumpSense product uses sophisticated algorithms to sense and predict sump pump problems.

I designed the PumpSense website, designed their logo, did their branding, and am in the process of designing their installation experience - which involves elements of hardware as well as software design. A full case study of both the website and experience design is in progress.
