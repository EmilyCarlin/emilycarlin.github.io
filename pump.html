Background

"PumpSense" is a tool made by a Boulder, Colorado based startup called Boulder Automation. If you are a homeowner with a basement, you probably know that your sump pump is what stands between your basement and flooding. Pump Sense is a sensor that monitors electrical current and water level in your sump pump and alerts you if there is a problem or if there may be a problem soon, enabling you to take action and hopefully prevent flooding.

I designed the PumpSense website, designed their logo, did their branding, and am in the process of designing their installation experience - which involves elements of hardware as well as software design.
